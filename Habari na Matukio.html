The Role of National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA)

The National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA) is a government institution responsible for overseeing the administration of all national examinations in Tanzania, including Matokeo Darasa la Saba 2023/2024. NECTA’s objectives are to assess students’ knowledge, skills, and attitudes, as well as certify their qualifications. The council ensures that examinations are conducted fairly, accurately, and efficiently, adhering to all examination regulations and procedures. NECTA plays a crucial role in overseeing the examination process, which takes place in the second week of September every year, and ensuring the integrity and reliability of the results.

Examined Subjects and Examination Formats in PSLE

The Matokeo Darasa la Saba 2023/2024 includes subjects such as Hisabati (Mathematics), English Language, Sayansi (Science), Maarifa ya Jamii (Social Studies) and Kiswahili. The examination formats for this national examination administered by the National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA) include multiple-choice questions, short answer questions, and practical examinations. It aims to assess students’ understanding, application, and analytical skills in various subjects, acquired through their primary education. These objectives of PSLE, as carried out by NECTA, are to assess learners’ skills and knowledge gained in different subjects, understand their basic skills and abilities, and identify students with the capacity to continue with secondary education and other training institutions.

Eligibility for the Examination

Students who have finished their primary school education are qualified to take the Matokeo Darasa la Saba 2023/2024 examination. Any pupil who has completed standard seven in either government or private schools can be enrolled and participate in this exam.

How to Check Matokeo Darasa La Saba 2023 Results

To access the Matokeo Darasa la Saba 2023/2024 results online, students can visit the official website of the National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA). Alternatively, our website offer the results, with the latter’s link available below for convenience. Follow steps below to access Standard Seven Examination Results

Step 1: Visit The Official NECTA Website

First, navigate to NECTA’s official website. Once on the homepage, locate the option to check results at the navigation bar at the top ribbon Click on this option to continue.

Step 2: Navigate to the Results Section

Once in the results section, the NECTA website will display various options such as Primary School Leaving Examination (PSLE), Form Two National Assessment (FTNA), and Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE). Select the PSLE option to proceed.

Step 3: Select Your Region and District

Results are organized by region and further subdivided by district. Select your region and district from the provided list to proceed.

Step 4: Search for Your Name

Once you have selected your district, a list of schools in that district will appear. Find your school from the provided list.

Step 5: Select Your School

The results are categorized by schools within the districts. Search for your name from the provided list. Once you find your name, you will be able to view your results.
